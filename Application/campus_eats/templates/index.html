Welcome to Campus eats database demo!

This site is not intended to be a full application. It merely demonstrates a sample user interface for the underlying database.

Admin

The admin view shows a top level view of driver and restaurant ratings so that an administrator can identify problem area or places/people that are succeeding.

Customer

Navigating to the customer view will automatically sign in as a random customer and present their most recent orders. Clicking on one of "rate driver" or "rate restaurant" will open a form that will allow a customer to leave or update their review.
